1 out of 4 car accidents in the US are caused by texting while driving. No amount of justification makes it okay.

A series of three posters were designed to raise awareness and support for not texting and driving. Aside from relaying facts to the public, the campaign strives to create a lasting impression by appealing to the audience emotionally. Stoplight colors were used to catch the attention of others. To keep viewers engaged, variations in hand drawn type were included.

Instructor: Veronica Vaughan
Date: Spring 2016
Category: Campaign + Illustration
